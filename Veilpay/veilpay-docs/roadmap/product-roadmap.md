# Product roadmap

Veilpay’s roadmap is organized around secure wallet foundations, private payments, fiat on/off ramps, and production operations.

## Completed foundations

- Consumer wallet app.
- Backend API.
- Multi-chain wallet support.
- Send and receive flows.
- Fiat ramp surfaces.
- WalletConnect.
- Security hardening.
- Observability and CI checks.

## Active and gated work

- Stellar SPP private XLM testnet flow.
- Native SPP proof generation and device readiness.
- WebSocket or streaming price updates.
- Indexer-based transaction-history expansion.
- Certificate pinning finalization.

## Future work

- Recurring and scheduled payments.
- Expanded fiat provider support.
- Privacy-chain tracks: Monero, Zcash, and Midnight.
